SPORTS

GULLI DANDA

Gulli Danda is a traditional bat-and-ball game that is enjoyed by children and adults alike in Kangra.

KITE FLYING

Kite flying is a popular traditional pastime in Kangra, especially during festivals like Makar Sankranti.

LANGDI

Langdi is a traditional Indian game similar to tag. It is a popular sport among young people in Kangra.

ARCHERY

Archery is a traditional sport in Kangra, and it is often practiced during local fairs and festivals.
