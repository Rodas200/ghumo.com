TRADITIONAL CLOTHES

PAHARI TOPI

These caps are typically made of wool or cotton and are embroidered with colorful threads, reflecting the local craftsmanship..

ODHAN

Women might also wear an "odhan" or a long scarf that covers their heads and shoulders..

KURTA PAJAMA

The traditional dress for men in Kangra typically includes a long tunic called a "kurta" and loose-fitting pants known as "pajama.

SILVER JEWELERY
Both men and women in Kangra may wear silver jewelry, including necklaces, earrings, and bangles.
