ARTS

BHARATHANATYAM

It is one of the classical dance forms of India and is widely regarded as one of the most ancient and traditional dance styles.

CARNATIC MUSIC

It is often referred to as Karnatic music, is one of the two major classical music traditions of India, the other being Hindustani classical music.

kOLAM

Kolam, also known as Rangoli or Muggulu, is a traditional and decorative art form practiced in South India, particularly in the states of Tamil Nadu.

SILAMBAM

Silambam is a traditional Indian martial art that originated in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India.
