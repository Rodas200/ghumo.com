TRAKKING

Mountain tracking, more commonly known as "mountain trekking" or "mountain hiking," refers to the activity of hiking or walking in the mountains and wilderness areas..

CAMPING

Camping is an outdoor recreational activity that involves spending time in natural environments, typically in tents or other temporary shelters, with the goal of enjoying the outdoors.

RIVER RAFTING

River rafting, also known as whitewater rafting, is an exciting outdoor adventure activity that involves navigating inflatable rafts through the turbulent waters of rivers and streams,.

MAUNTAIN BIKING

Mountain biking is an exhilarating outdoor activity that involves riding specialized bicycles designed for off-road terrain, including rugged trails, dirt paths, rocky terrain, and steep hills.
