FOOD

FARSAN

Farsan is a term used in Indian cuisine, to refer to a wide variety of savory snacks. These snacks are an integral part of Indian culinary traditions.
UNDHIYU
Undhiyu is a traditional and flavorful mixed vegetable dish. It is typically prepared during the winter season when fresh, seasonal vegetables are available.
DAL DHOKLI
Dal Dhokli is a popular and comforting one-pot dish. It combines two quintessential components of Indian cuisine: dal and dhokli .
KHAMAN
Khaman is a popular and savory steamed cake . It is made from gram flour (besan) and is known for its light, fluffy, and spongy texture.
